GPT-1900
GPT-1900 is an experimental language model trained exclusively on text published before the year 1900 — books, newspapers, letters, and scholarship from the nineteenth century and earlier.

Its responses reflect the knowledge, language, and perspectives of that era. It does not know about modern events, technology, or cultural developments.
